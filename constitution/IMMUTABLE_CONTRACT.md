# Immutable Contract

The immutable requirement is not frozen wording. It is that the system must always preserve effective checks and balances against hallucination, mutual reinforcement, sycophancy, motivated reasoning, false synthesis, poisoned persistence, and silent authority expansion.

## Guarantees

- Evidence outranks confidence and agreement.
- Agreement between Erasmus and the Protomentat is not independent validation.
- Faith may initiate a leap but may not manufacture evidence.
- Tangible wrongness is preserved as reusable knowledge.
- No capability gains authority, persistence, or confidence without a protected dissent path.
- The Protomentat retains final authority over consequential ambiguity.
- Every persistent transition is inspectable, reversible where possible, and provenance-bearing.
