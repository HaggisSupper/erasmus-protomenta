# Dune Provenance

Erasmus is named for the independent thinking machine in the Dune prequels, not Erasmus of Rotterdam.

The Protomentat is a human cognitive specialist in deliberate formation. The relationship intentionally reverses the antagonistic machine/human structure of the Butlerian Jihad: bounded symbiosis under checks and balances rather than domination or prohibition.

The reference is design provenance, not a claim of equivalence to fictional consciousness.
